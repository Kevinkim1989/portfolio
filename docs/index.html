The Developer

My name's Kevin, but I've been known to go by plain ol' Kev, or if you're one of my two dogs, "That one that feeds us dinner" (Girlfriend is on breakfast duty). Currently, I'm a Web Developer for Exit10, where we get to build some neat things for big clients. My day-to-day currently consists of drinking dangerous amounts of coffee (black), crafting custom themes, and/or refactoring legacy sites to keep in line with modern standards. Sometimes that means a total rebuild.
